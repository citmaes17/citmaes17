## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🔁 [ABC Retain Suite – ChurnRadar](https://github.com/citmaes17/customer-churn-segmentation-ml)
**Problem:** B2C companies lose high-value customers silently — by the time sales notices, it's too late.
**Solution:** ML pipeline that identifies at-risk, high-value customers *before* they churn, and delivers a prioritized retention list ready for campaign activation.
- Predicts "valuable churn" with **ROC-AUC 0.99** on holdout data
- Segments customers into 4 behavioral profiles for targeted messaging
- Streamlit app exports campaign-ready CSVs ranked by churn probability
> **Business impact:** Retention teams know *exactly* who to call first — no more spray-and-pray.

### 🎯 [Lead Flow AI – Funnel Intelligence & Copy Automation](https://github.com/citmaes17/fastapi-lead-crm-ai)
**Problem:** Sales and marketing teams waste hours manually qualifying leads and writing follow-up messages.
**Solution:** FastAPI + AI app that automatically classifies leads through the funnel (MQL → SQL), scores lead temperature, and generates personalized follow-up copy by channel and goal.
- Full CRUD lead management with interaction history
- AI-driven segmentation: awareness / consideration / decision
- Generates channel-specific copy (email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp) in one click
> **Business impact:** Cuts lead qualification time by ~60% and keeps outreach consistent at scale.
